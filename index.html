Our Little Farm

A small homestead just getting started — chickens scratching in the yard, a garden taking shape, and big plans for goats down the road.

🐔 Chickens 🌱 Garden — coming soon 🐐 Goats — on the wishlist

Welcome! We're a small family farm tucked into Waterloo, Indiana, taking things one project at a time. Right now our flock of chickens runs the show, but we're laying the groundwork (literally) for a garden this season — and dreaming about adding a few goats once the fences and shelter are sorted.

This page is our home base for sharing how things grow — check back as we add photos, updates, and more.
